Our Signature Candles

Beeswax Candle

Made from natural beeswax, these candles emit a warm, honey-like aroma and burn cleanly, making them eco-friendly and beautiful.

Soy Candle

Crafted from soybean wax, a renewable resource that burns cleaner and longer than paraffin, and available in a variety of colors and scents.

Gel Candle

Made from mineral oil and polymer resin, the transparent gel allows decorative objects like seashells or glitter to be embedded for a unique effect.

Coconut Wax Candle

Derived from the meat of coconuts through hydrogenation, this wax is creamy, natural, and burns smoothly with a light scent throw.

Palm Wax Candle

Made from palm oil through refining and hydrogenation, palm wax produces crystalline patterns and offers a clean, firm burn.

Canola Wax Candle

Derived from canola oil, this biodegradable, vegetable-based wax is eco-friendly and offers a soft burn profile ideal for blending.

Rice Bran Wax Candle

Extracted from rice bran during oil production, this plant-based wax is eco-conscious and used standalone or in blends for smooth burning.

About Flamora

At Flamora, each candle is crafted with care and intention. We use natural soy wax, lead-free wicks, and premium essential oils to ensure our candles are eco-friendly, safe, and long-lasting.
